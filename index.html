About us

Technological equipment Čokoland deals with the distribution and sale of industrial equipment in the territory of the European Union. The headquarters of the company is located in Prelog, Croatia. Technological equipment Čokoland is part of the corporation Čokoland d.o.o. which has been operating successfully for over 12 years. Together with our business partners and customers, we build a permanent relationship, providing them with quality and safety. Our goal is to successfully implement complete business solutions and we are dedicated to our partners to enable them to achieve excellence in their business activities.
